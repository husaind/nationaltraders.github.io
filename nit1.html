Director's Message

Welcome to NIT City

National Institute of Technology, (Deemed University) is a premier higher education institute located in the southern state of Telangana. Established in 1959 as the first of the RECs, it has evolved into a top-tier technological university.

Our mission is to provide high-quality technical education, foster research and development, and incubate entrepreneurship. We are committed to shaping young minds into ethical, skilled, and innovative professionals ready to tackle global challenges.

With state-of-the-art facilities, a distinguished faculty, and a vibrant campus culture, NIT continues to be a beacon of knowledge and progress.
